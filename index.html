UCI Programming Club

Welcome to the Fall 2014 UCI Programming Club Website!

We are a small group of programmers and soon-to-be programmers at UCI. Most of us are graduate students in neuroscience. Each quarter we start from the basics and teach each other how to program. We also help each other solve programming problems in our labs by sharing code and ideas.
