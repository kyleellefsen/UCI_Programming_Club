UCI Programming Club

Welcome to the Fall 2014 UCI Programming Club Website!

We are a small group of programmers and soon-to-be programmers at UCI. We are postdocs, grad students, lab techs, and undergrads. Anyone is welcome to attend. Most of us are in neuroscience labs. Each quarter we start from the basics and teach each other how to program. We also help each other solve programming problems in our labs by sharing code and ideas.

This Quarter (Fall 2014) we meet at 12 PM on Mondays in Gross Hall Room 2005
